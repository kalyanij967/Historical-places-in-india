Historical Places to Visit in India


Taj Mahal

Taj Mahal is the epitome of grandeur and splendour.A UNESCO World Heritage Site, it is located on the bank of the Yamuna River and is counted among the most popular heritage monuments in India.

Golden Temple

Golden temple, also known as Harminder Sahib, is the most important religious place for Sikhs and also the main attraction in Amritsar. The temple or gurdwara is a symbol for equality and brotherhood.

Qutub Minar

At a height of 234 feet, this minaret is the tallest individual tower in the world and is a perfect display of Mughal architectural brilliance.Qutub Minar is the tallest individual tower in the world and second tallest monument of Delhi.

Hawa Mahal

The Hawa Mahal stands at intersection of the main road in Jaipur. It is regarded as the signature building of Jaipur.Hawa Mahal's unique attraction is its 953 windows that cover the lace-like walls.

India Gate

India Gate, official name Delhi Memorial, originally called All-India War Memorial, monumental sandstone arch in New Delhi.The India Gate, is a war memorial located in New Delhi. It is dedicated to the soldiers, who died during the First World War and the Third Anglo-Afghan War.

Hampi

Hampi, the city of ruins, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Situated in shadowed depth of hills and valleys in Karnataka, surrounded ancient monuments, beautiful temples, bustling street markets, treasury building and captivating remains of Vijayanagar Empire.

Mysore Palace

The former residence of the royal family of Mysore, the Mysore Palace is an extremely breath-taking example of the Indo-Saracenic style of architecture. It was built in the year 1912 for the 24th Ruler of the Wodeyar Dynasty and is till date one of the biggest palaces in the country.

Shore Temple

The Shore Temple lies in front of the Bay of Bengal, hence it is known by the name mentioned.The structured temple is made out of granite chunks. The temple was constructed in such a way that it would overlook the Bay of Bengal at a Southern Chennai village called Mahabalipuram.
